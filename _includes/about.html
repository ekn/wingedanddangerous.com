About

Elisabeth Nicula


I'm a San Francisco–based graphic designer specializing in illustration and brand management across web and print media. I work with clients big and small, near and far.

I like breakfast, solving design puzzles, and looking at things through binoculars.

Art


Take a look at my fine art at elisabethnicula.com or on Tumblr. I am a member of the printmaking cooperative Graphic Arts Workshop here in San Francisco, and make etchings, woodcuts, and paintings that reside in public and private collections.
