Berklee.edu Redesign

About the Berklee.edu Redesign

Client: Berklee College of Music
Role: concept, design, information design

My interim redesign of the berklee.edu home page, launched in October 2011 and replaced by a full migration to Drupal in August 2012, garnered increased traffic to key action-oriented pages—an 81 percent increase to the Admissions home page, 44 percent increase to the Admissions audition and interview page, and a 14 percent increase to the Summer Programs home page—and decreased the bounce rate by 12 percent.
