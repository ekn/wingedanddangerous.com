About

Elisabeth Nicula

I'm a graphic designer specializing in illustration and brand management across web and print media. My current gig is at Berklee College of Music. I like clean, confident design, breakfast, and looking at things through binoculars.

Art


Take a look at my fine art over at elisabethnicula.com. It's fine. I make etchings, woodcuts, and paintings that reside in at least one public collection and many private ones. Dig Boston recently featured my bitty woodcut Wish You Were Here.
