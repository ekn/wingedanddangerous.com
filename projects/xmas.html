About

Elisabeth Nicula

I am a graphic designer specializing in illustration and brand management across web and print media. My current gig is at Berklee College of Music.

Take a look at my fine art over at elisabethnicula.com. It's pretty fine. I make etchings, woodcuts, and paintings that reside in at least one public collection and many private ones, including, but not limited to, my mom's.

I may be available for your design project, particularly if it involves illustration, responsive web design, or identity design; art, bikes, or outer space. Drop me a line at elisabeth@wingedanddangerous.com.
