The Senefelder Club of the North Country

About The Senefelder Club of the North Country

Client: Roger Bailey
Role: design, development, and content strategy

The Senefelder Club of the North Country is the studio of master printmaker and teacher Roger Bailey. His working friendship with American realist painter Jack Beal and sculptor/painter Sondra Freckelton led to a body of intaglio and lithographic prints that emphasizes the collaborative relationship of artist and printer.

The responsive design optimizes for mobile, tablet, and full-sized browsers to showcase the work they made together.
