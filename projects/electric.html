Electric Industry Infographic

About the Electric Industry Infographic

Client: Tufts University Graduate Student Team
Role: information design and illustration

Created for a Metropolitan Area Planning Council report, this graphic explains the structure of the electric industry in Massachusetts. Before the 1997 restructuring act deregulated the electricity market, investor-owned utilities were vertically integrated monopolies handling electricity supply, transmission, and distribution. The restructuring act required utilities to sell the supply side of their business and buy power from the open market.
